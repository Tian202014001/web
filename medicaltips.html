At Home
It is equally important for nurses to be vigilant at home. They must remain at home when ill to keep infections out of the healthcare environment. It is a good idea for nurses to have more than one pair of work shoes on rotation and to leave these in the car when arriving home. If they need to be washed, make sure to effectively clean your hands before and after touching them. Also, for non-work-related activities, you should have a separate pair of shoes. Before interacting with your family, take off your work clothes and have a shower. Wash and sanitize those clothes as soon as possible.
